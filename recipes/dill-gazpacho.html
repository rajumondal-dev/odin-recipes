Dill Gazpacho

Description

Dill Gazpacho is a refreshing and vibrant cold soup that blends the crispness of cucumbers, the mild sweetness of tomatoes, and the zesty brightness of fresh dill. A twist on the traditional Spanish gazpacho, this variation introduces a herbal, aromatic layer that enhances its cooling effect—perfect for hot summer days. Garlic, onion, and a splash of lemon or vinegar add depth and tang, while olive oil gives it a silky texture. Served chilled, this no-cook soup is both nutritious and hydrating, making it a light yet flavorful choice for a healthy starter or a revitalizing mid-day meal.

Main Ingredients:

  • 2 medium cucumbers (peeled and chopped)
  • 2 ripe tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 small red or white onion (chopped)
  • 1 clove garlic (minced)
  • 1 tablespoon fresh dill (finely chopped)
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice or white wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste
  • 1-2 slices of stale bread (optional, for thickness)
  • ¼ cup cold water (adjust for desired consistency)

Optional Garnishes:

  • Chopped fresh dill
  • Diced cucumber or tomato
  • A drizzle of olive oil
  • Croutons or yogurt swirl

Steps

1. Prepare the vegetables:

  • Peel and chop the cucumbers.
  • Chop the tomatoes and onion.
  • Mince the garlic.
  • Tear the bread into small pieces (if using).

2. Blend the base:

  • In a blender or food processor, combine cucumbers, tomatoes, onion, garlic, dill, bread (optional), and cold
    • water.
  • Blend until smooth.

3. Season and emulsify:

  • Add lemon juice (or vinegar), olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  • Blend again until the mixture is fully combined and creamy.

4. Adjust the consistency:

  • If too thick, add a little more cold water until you get your desired texture.

5. Chill the soup:

  • Transfer to a bowl or container.
  • Cover and refrigerate for at least 1-2 hours to let the flavors meld and the soup chill thoroughly.

6. Serve:

  • Stir before serving.
  • Pour into bowls and garnish with extra dill, diced cucumber or tomato, olive oil drizzle, or croutons if
    • desired.
